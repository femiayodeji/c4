Hackathon

Code Can Conquer Crime (C4)

This hackathon seeks to provide viable solutions to crime in Oyo State. Participants would participate in a week long programme were each day a milestone has to be accomplished and forwarded to the organizers and on the last day the participants would be asked to pitch what they have worked on.

Instruction

The hackathon will be completed in stages. A total of three stages and each stage has an expected deliverable (document) to be submitted to c4@nacossui.org. Participants would be notified accordingly via email.

Stage 1: Registration and proposal submission

Send a proposal about your idea. Deadline - 14th January, 2020 12:00AM

Stage 2: Methodology and Design

Send a document explaining your design and how you intend to go about turning your idea into a minimum viable product including the technologies you intend to use. Deadline - 15th January, 2020 12:00AM

Stage 3: Business Model

Send a drafted business plan for your solution. Include your target market segment and size and how do you intend to acquire users. Additional information related to your solution is also encouraged. Deadline - 16th January, 2020 12:00AM

Stage 4: The Hackathon

The selected participants would be invited for a 24-hour hackathon where they would be provided with resources to continue building their solutions. Deadline - 17th January, 2020 10:00AM

Objectives

Our Criteria

Entries will be judged by a panel of experts against the following criteria.

Uniqueness & Creativity

We expect all participants idea to be original and unique. For existing ideas, there should be significant improvement with competitive advantage to the proposed system.

Feasibility

Proposed project/system/product should be achievable and realistic or if it is a daunting solution, participants are to work on a Minimum Viable Product(MVP).

Teamwork

We expect participants of the hackathon to work in teams. We recommend at least a team of two and a maximum of three.
